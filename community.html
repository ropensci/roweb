Community

rOpenSci is entirely a community effort. The project originally came together from conversations between like-minded researchers and continues to grow as more people contribute tools, expertise and research use cases. We are now a fast growing group of researchers, students, software developers, and others working towards improving the world of science. We welcome you to join the rOpenSci community and help us improve tools and practices available to researchers.

How to join our community

If there is any project that excites you, please feel free to offer suggestions for improvement, new use cases, or fork a copy and send a pull request. We welcome new collaborators even on our efforts.

If you have an idea for a new package (this could be a new data source, a new tool to improve reproducibility, or something we haven't thought of yet), and maybe even want to learn how to develop one with our help, please drop us a note. We would love to hear from you.

We are always looking for reports, use cases, and stories from our users. If you'd like to write a package tutorial or example, we would love to post in on our website and share it with other researchers.

If you already use and love any of our packages, we encourage you to give a talk or workshop at your institution. We may even be able to provide you with some funds for refreshments, and travel to help with your outreach efforts. Ask us about what support we can offer.
